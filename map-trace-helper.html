Trace Helper

Drag the dots on the map. The coordinate list on the right updates live, so you can manually refine the route and copy it back into the game.

How To Use It

Click a point to select it, then drag it or use the nudge buttons for tiny moves. Add point inserts one after the selected point. Copy JS gives you a ready-to-paste GUIDE_ROUTE_POINTS block.
